About

Software Community. Driven by you, supported by YorkDevelopers.

York.Developers provide coding meetups for Developers based in (and around) York. We are a group of dedicated software professionals who lead by example and meet often to share knowledge and experiences in software engineering.

We decided that we wanted to enable more Software Developers in York to share our growth as a team and provide the one place you need to see what is happening in York - right now.
