York Developers support, develop and encourage a community of Software Developers in (and around) the City of York. We promote the values of learning and collaboration, helping to develop individuals and groups.

We focus on the progression of people, who we hope will assist others in their learning journey.

We believe this empowerment of others will support the need for technological and personal advancement in a wider context. We provide the framework for those involved to determine a goal and drive towards it through innovation and collaboration.

We are a non-profit organisation.
